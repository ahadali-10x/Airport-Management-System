Our Services

Medical & Emergency

Facilities are available for all privileged needy passengers and senior citizens. Facilities are signposted and identified with the standard International Sinology. Passenger with such needs should notify their airlines ahead of their visit. They can contact concerned airlines for wheel chair service on their special counters in arrival and departure Halls. For such facility Civil Aviation Authority Duty Terminal Manager Office/Duty Facilitation Office can also be contacted.

Passenger Facilitation Services (PFS)

Baggage is the most cumbersome part of traveling; especially if you’re traveling in a large group or with children and you all need to take care your stuff. AIIAP offers a range of services to make things easier for you. Porters can help with luggage from DROP-LANE to CHECK-IN counters and from BAGGAGE CLAIM to PICK-UP LANE. If you’re departing, you’ll find the Porter Service Desk at the concourse hall. Alternatively you’ll find them near the Baggage area at Arrival Lounges. For assistance please contact CAA Facilitation Desk. Charges

Guidlines

Guidelines for Passengers

Following are the general guidelines for passengers:

  1. Do not carry any banned / restricted item / goods on board. Contact concerned airline or its representative in this regard.
  2. Strictly adhere to the baggage weight limit policy and other instructions written on the Airline Ticket.
  3. Do Not Repeat Do not place your valuable items, for example currency, jewelry, Laptop, mobile phone, tablet computer, camera, watch etc, in your check-in baggage during departure.
  4. Do not take any item, eatable, tea / drink etc from anyone / unknown person.
  5. Do not give / handover your hand carry bag / item to anyone.
  6. Do not forget to pick / collect your baggage after search / screening from customs, ANF and ASF.
  7. Obtain necessary information regarding ticket / journey from airline representative at the time of arriving of boarding passes.
  8. Register your complaint regarding loss, damage or theft of / from baggage immediately to Airline or its representative on arrival at Destination.
  9. Do Not Repeat Do not forget to bring your CNIC.
  10. Kindly take care of your luggage.
  11. International Overseas / Pakistani Passengers will not submit embarkation / disembarkation form on board or at international airports at Pakistan.

Guidelines of Import / Availability of Liquor

Import of Liquor
Import and transportation of liquor is totally banned in Islamic Republic of Pakistan and will be confiscated at the arrival lounge. However, the non- Muslim can avail this facility from designated places and Hotels on permit basis.
Please note that drinking at public places is prohibited.
